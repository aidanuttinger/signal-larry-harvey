## Project Overview: Legacy Architect

A proprietary, time-locked messaging platform built exclusively for HFG's clients. Allows high-net-worth families to record video, voice, and written messages that unlock at specific life events (inheritance, wedding, graduation, milestone birthdays).

**The Opportunity:** HFG becomes the only wealth management practice in the world with a proprietary legacy technology platform — differentiating the practice and retaining heir relationships across generational wealth transfers.

**Why HFG:** 70% of heirs change advisors after inheriting. Legacy Architect solves this by anchoring heirs emotionally to the family's values and their relationship with HFG, *before* the inheritance event.
