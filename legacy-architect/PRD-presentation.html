Legacy Architect

Where Love Defeats Time
Product Requirements Document | March 16, 2026

Product Vision

Legacy Architect is the platform where love defeats time. It gives people the ability to speak to the people they love most — not just now, but at the exact moment those people need to hear it most.

When they graduate. When they marry. When they hold their first child. When they stand at an inheritance threshold wondering what their family truly valued.

In an era where technology has accelerated human disconnection and the average person dies without having said the most important things, Legacy Architect becomes the infrastructure for emotional continuity across generations.

For wealth managers, it is the relationship bridge that turns a transactional inheritance event into a moment of profound family alignment.

For families, it is the archive of everything that should never be lost.

The Problem

B2C: The Absence Problem

Most people die having never said the things that mattered most. Not because they didn't feel them — but because they didn't know how, didn't think they had time, or assumed there would be a better moment later.

  • Children grow up without access to a grandparent's voice.
  • Parents miss the chance to speak to a child at their wedding.
  • Wisdom accumulated over a lifetime dissolves within a generation.

Existing solutions are inadequate:

  • Writing a letter is analog and fragile
  • Video recordings sit unwatched on hard drives
  • Legal wills are transactional, cold, and contested

There is no platform purpose-built for the emotional, time-specific delivery of a person's most important words.

B2B: The Wealth Transfer Crisis

90%
of inherited wealth changes advisors within 2 years of transfer

Wealth managers face a structural crisis. The relationship is with the parent — not the heir. When the parent dies, the heir takes the money elsewhere.

There is no good tool for advisors to bridge that generational relationship proactively. Generic CRM outreach is cold. Estate planning documents are clinical.

Legacy Architect gives wealth managers a high-intimacy, differentiated service offering: a family legacy vault that anchors the heir emotionally to the family's values and, by extension, to the advisor who helped build it.

Core Features

1. Message Recording

Record in four formats: video (up to 10 min), voice, written letter (rich text), and photo/document uploads. All media is encrypted and stored — with mirrors to Arweave for permanent archival.

2. Unlock Triggers (The Delivery Engine)

Each message is assigned one or more triggers that determine when it becomes available to the recipient:

  • Date trigger: "December 25, 2040"
  • Age trigger: "when Maya turns 18"
  • Life event trigger: graduation, wedding, birth of first child, 18th birthday, inheritance event, death of creator
  • Custom event: (P2) user-defined text triggers

3. Time Collapse ⭐ (The Unique Differentiator)

Record a message explicitly framed as: "I am [age] today. This message is for you when you are [same age]."

A 42-year-old father records a message to his daughter to be opened when she turns 42 — so she can hear from him as a peer, not as a parent, at the exact same chapter of life.

This is what no competitor has. This is what makes Legacy Architect unique.

4. Family Vault (Multi-Generational Archive)

A shared vault where grandparents, parents, aunts, uncles, and siblings can all contribute. The vault becomes the family's institutional memory — passed down indefinitely across generations.

5. Guardian / Succession System (Trust Architecture)

Every creator designates 1–3 trusted guardians who:

  • Confirm life events ("My son got married")
  • Vote on emergency releases (2-of-3 required)
  • Receive annual check-in pings from the system

6. Emergency Release System (30-Day Challenge Window)

If a creator is deceased or incapacitated:

  1. Guardians vote to initiate emergency release
  2. System notifies creator via email (30-day challenge window)
  3. If no response: emergency messages release to recipients
  4. All events logged to blockchain for auditability

7. AI Message Coach (P2)

Users often freeze when faced with a blank recording screen. The AI Coach asks guided questions to help them structure their thoughts before hitting record — without writing the message for them.

"What's a moment with [Name] you'll never forget?" → "What do you wish you could be there to see?" → "What's the most important thing you've learned?"

8. Legacy Gifts (P3) — Physical + Digital

Attach a curated physical gift (jewelry, keepsakes, books, custom items) to a message. When the trigger fires, the recipient receives both the video/letter AND the physical gift — shipped to their current address.

Example: A grandmother sends both a gold locket AND a personal video message on her granddaughter's 18th birthday.

15-20% commission on gift GMV.

9. Wealth Manager Dashboard (B2B MVP)

A dedicated portal where advisors can:

  • View all their clients' vault statuses in one place
  • Initiate vault creation on behalf of clients
  • Get notified of upcoming inheritance events (60 days out)
  • See vault health metrics (message count, sealed status)

Key constraint: Advisors can see vault metadata but NEVER message content. Content is private to creator. This is a hard privacy rule.

Technical Architecture

Frontend

Next.js 14+ web + React Native mobile (iOS + Android)

Backend

Node.js / NestJS REST + WebSocket API

Storage

PostgreSQL (metadata), AWS S3 / Cloudflare R2 (media), Arweave (permanent archive)

Blockchain

Polygon (delivery instructions, guardian votes, unlock conditions, audit trail)

Security

  • E2E Encryption: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Identity Verification: Email OTP + optional MFA
  • Minor Protection: Creator designates minors; requires guardian confirmation before unlock
  • Audit Logging: All privileged actions logged immutably
