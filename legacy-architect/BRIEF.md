# Legacy Architect — Project Brief

## Overview
An app that allows people to leave messages for loved ones across time — video, voice, letters, and physical gifts — unlocked by specific life events, dates, or ages.

## The Core Idea
"Collapse time" — record wisdom and emotional connection today, delivered at the exact right moment in the future. A digital ethical will. A family legacy vault.

## Two Customer Segments
1. **B2C — Individuals & Families** — Anyone who wants to leave messages for children, grandchildren, future loved ones
2. **B2B — Wealth Managers** — HFG Wealth Management (Larry Harvey) is the pilot client. Advisors use it to preserve heir relationships and reinforce family legacy values around inheritance events.

## Key Features
- Video, voice, letter, photo/document uploads
- Unlock triggers: specific date, recipient age, life event (wedding, graduation, 18th birthday, birth of first child, inheritance event)
- Time Collapse feature: record for a recipient at the same age you are now
- Family Vault: multi-generational archive of wisdom
- Emergency release: guardian consensus system (2 of 3 vote)
- AI message coaching: guided prompts to help users articulate what matters
- Legacy Gifts: physical gifts + message delivered simultaneously on unlock date (15-20% commission model)

## Unique Differentiators
- Blockchain-backed longevity (Arweave for media, on-chain for delivery instructions)
- Time Collapse feature (emotionally unique — no competitor has this)
- Wealth manager B2B integration
- Multi-generational family vault
- AI-coached message recording
- Physical gift delivery alongside message

## Monetization
- B2C: monthly/annual subscription, tiered by storage
- B2B: licensed to wealth managers (pilot: Larry Harvey / HFG Wealth Management)
- Revenue share: 15-20% commission on Legacy Gift orders

## First B2B Client
Larry Harvey — HFG Wealth Management — already in conversation. This is the primary pilot case study.

## Legal Positioning
"Digital Ethical Will" — companion to a legal will. Not legally binding but morally binding. Reduces family conflict. Carries emotional weight that legal documents cannot.

## Technical Approach
- Frontend: Next.js web + React Native mobile
- Backend: Node.js / NestJS
- Storage: Arweave (permanent media), PostgreSQL (metadata), Object storage (temp processing)
- Delivery: Email + push notifications + secure unlock system
- Security: E2E encryption, identity verification, executor authorization
- Blockchain: Polygon (delivery instructions, guardian assignments, unlock conditions)

## Guardian / Succession System
- Creator appoints 1-3 trusted guardians at setup
- 2-of-3 consensus required for emergency release
- Annual check-in ping; missed check-in triggers executor notification
- 30-day challenge window before emergency messages release
- Recipients loaded into system once they exist

## Competitive White Space
No competitor has: wealth manager B2B, time collapse feature, multi-gen vault, blockchain longevity guarantee, AI coaching, physical gift delivery.

Closest competitor: SafeBeyond (weaker, limited triggers, no B2B, declining).
