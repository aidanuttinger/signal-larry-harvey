Legacy Architect

The Time-Collapse Messaging Platform for Generational Legacy

B2C + B2B App

The Core Concept

Legacy Architect lets people record video, voice, and letter messages for loved ones that unlock at meaningful life events — graduation, wedding, inheritance, turning 18, milestone birthdays.

The Core Insight
High-net-worth founders and families want to leave more than money. They want to leave guidance, love, and values that persist for generations. But they don't have a system to do it. We built one.

Messages are stored on Arweave blockchain (100-year guarantee). Not dependent on a company staying alive. Not dependent on anyone's cloud account. Truly permanent.

Three Revenue Streams

1. B2C: Personal Legacy Accounts

Direct-to-Consumer

Anyone can create a legacy vault for themselves and family.

  • Free tier: 3 messages, basic triggers
  • Premium ($99/year): Unlimited messages, custom triggers, physical gift delivery, AI message coaching
  • Premium+ ($299/year): Multi-generational vault, family consensus system, guardian management

2. B2B: Wealth Manager Licensing

Wealth Managers + Family Offices

Advisors offer Legacy Architect to their high-net-worth clients as a premium service.

  • The Problem They Solve: Heirs get the money but lose the connection to the person who left it. Advisors become irrelevant after the transfer.
  • The Solution: Heirs keep receiving messages from the advisor's original client → stays connected to the advisor → doesn't switch firms.
  • Licensing Model: $500–$2K/month per advisor (based on AUM). White-label or co-branded.
  • Commission: 15–20% of premium upgrades (when clients pay directly)

3. Legacy Gifts: Physical Delivery

Premium Add-On Service

When a message triggers, we can print and ship it with physical gifts.

  • Handwritten letter printed on custom stationery + shipped on trigger date
  • Paired with gift products (custom book, jewelry, keepsake)
  • Premium service: $50–$200 per delivery
  • High margin, high touch experience

What Makes This Different

⏱️ Time Collapse

Messages unlock at specific life events (graduation, wedding, inheritance), not arbitrary dates. Incredibly powerful moment of connection.

🔒 Blockchain Permanence

Stored on Arweave. 100-year guarantee. Not dependent on any single company's survival. True digital immortality.

🤖 AI Message Coaching

Record raw video → AI transcribes, edits, coaches for maximum emotional impact. The message gets better, not artificial.

👥 Guardian Consensus

Multi-person approval system for sensitive messages. Heirs' guardians can manage what gets shared and when.

Legal Positioning: Digital Ethical Will

Legacy Architect is not a replacement for a legal will — it's a companion to it.

The Legal Strategy

  • Legal Will: Property, money, assets (handled by attorneys, executed once)
  • Ethical Will: Values, stories, wisdom (emotional legacy, ongoing)
  • Digital Ethical Will: What Legacy Architect is — the technology to deliver the ethical will across time
  • Our Position: "Complement to the legal estate plan, not a replacement. Used alongside, not instead of, traditional estate planning."

This keeps us out of legal trouble while positioning the product as the premium, high-touch companion to traditional estate planning.
