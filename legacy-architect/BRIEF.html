Legacy Architect

Leave messages across time. Collapse the future into now.

The Core Idea

Record wisdom and emotional connection today, delivered at the exact right moment in the future. A digital ethical will. A family legacy vault.

What It Does

An app that allows people to leave messages for loved ones across time.
  • Video, voice, letters, physical gifts
  • Unlock triggers: specific dates, recipient age, life events (wedding, graduation, 18th birthday, inheritance)
  • Time Collapse: record for a recipient at the same age you are now
  • Multi-generational family vault

Two Customer Segments

B2C — Individuals & Families

Anyone who wants to leave messages for children, grandchildren, future loved ones. Monthly/annual subscription, tiered by storage.

B2B — Wealth Managers

HFG Wealth Management (Larry Harvey) is the pilot. Advisors use it to preserve heir relationships and reinforce family legacy values. Licensed model.

Key Features

  • Emergency Release: Guardian consensus system (2 of 3 vote)
  • AI Message Coaching: Guided prompts to help users articulate what matters
  • Legacy Gifts: Physical gifts + message delivered simultaneously (15-20% commission)
  • Blockchain-backed Longevity: Arweave for media permanence
  • Multi-generational Vault: Wisdom across families and time

Unique Differentiators

  • Time Collapse feature (no competitor has this)
  • Wealth manager B2B integration
  • AI-coached message recording
  • Physical gift delivery alongside message
  • Blockchain permanence guarantee
  • Guardian succession system

Why This Wins

Closest competitor: SafeBeyond (weaker, limited triggers, no B2B, declining)

Legacy Architect has what the market has never seen: wealth manager B2B, time collapse, multi-gen vault, blockchain longevity, AI coaching, physical gift delivery.

Pilot Client

Larry Harvey — HFG Wealth Management. Already in conversation. Primary case study for B2B model validation.

Technical Foundation

Frontend

Next.js web + React Native mobile

Backend

Node.js / NestJS

Storage

Arweave (permanent), PostgreSQL (metadata), Object storage

Security

E2E encryption, identity verification, Polygon blockchain

Monetization

  • B2C: Monthly/annual subscription (storage-tiered)
  • B2B: Licensed to wealth managers
  • Revenue share: 15-20% commission on Legacy Gift orders

Legal Positioning

"Digital Ethical Will" — companion to a legal will. Not legally binding but morally binding. Reduces family conflict. Carries emotional weight that legal documents cannot.
