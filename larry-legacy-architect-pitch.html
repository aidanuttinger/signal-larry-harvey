Section 3B — Product Deep Dive

Nine core features.
Built to last forever.

Every feature in Legacy Architect is designed around one principle: messages that survive the company, survive the platform, and survive time itself.

1

Message Recording

Record in four formats: video (up to 10 min), voice, written letter (rich text editor), photo/document uploads. All encrypted at rest. All mirrored to Arweave for permanent archival.

2

Smart Unlock Triggers

Date-based ("December 25, 2040") • Age-based ("when they turn 18") • Life event ("wedding," "graduation," "birth of first child," "inheritance event") • Custom events • Multiple triggers per message (unlock on whichever comes first)

3

Time Collapse™ (Unique)

Record yourself at age 42 for your child to receive when they turn 42. Hear from your parent as a peer, at the same chapter of life. No competitor has this. This is what makes Legacy Architect different.

4

Family Vault

Multi-generational shared archive. Grandparents, parents, siblings contribute. Vault grows across decades. Messages pass to heirs. Becomes the family's institutional memory.

5

Guardian / Succession System

Creator appoints 1–3 trusted guardians. Guardians confirm life events, vote on emergency releases (2-of-3 required), receive annual check-ins. Guardian role is social, not legal.

6

Emergency Release System

If creator is deceased or incapacitated: guardians vote → system notifies creator (30-day challenge window) → if no response, messages release to recipients. All logged to blockchain.

7

AI Message Coach (Phase 2)

Many users freeze with a blank screen. Coach asks guided questions ("What moment do you never forget?" "What do you wish you could see?") → structures thoughts → user records in their own words.

8

Legacy Gifts (Phase 3)

Curated marketplace of gifts (jewelry, keepsakes, books, custom items). Attach to a message. When trigger fires, gift + message arrive together. 15–20% commission on GMV.

9

Wealth Manager Dashboard (B2B)

Advisor provisioning, client vault management, event alerts (60 days before inheritance events), vault health reporting. Advisors see metadata — never message content.
