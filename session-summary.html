Where It All Began

The Story That Built Everything

In 1985, a young man from Tampa walked into the financial services industry — not chasing money, but chasing something harder to name. What he was really chasing was the chance to make wealth mean something.

"I wanted to build a firm where I could impact as many lives as possible — not just manage their money, but honor what it represents."
Larry Harvey started in 1985 with Lutheran Brotherhood — a faith-based financial institution — and rose quickly to management, serving as Estate and Charitable Planning Specialist for the entire southeast region. It wasn't a career path. It was a formation. By the time he founded Harvey Financial Group in 1998 and launched HFG Wealth Management in 2008, he had 23 years of evidence that wealth managed without values is wealth that eventually costs you everything that matters.

HFG isn't a wealth management firm that happens to be faith-based. It's a legacy firm that happens to manage wealth. That distinction — held quietly for 35 years — is the single most powerful market differentiator in the industry today. We're going to make it heard.
